Samsung A72

Samsung A72 is a mid-range phone belonging to the a series variant of samsung mobile. The device was launched on March 17,2021. Samsung a72 comes with two different storage capacities, the 128gb and 256gb. The phone has alot of exciting features you may need from a smartphone and looks flashy.

Design

Samsung a72 is a smartphone refurbished at different choice of colour for users. The phone come in awesome blue,black,white and violet colour.The device weighs 203g(7.16oz) with a glass front(Gorilla glass 5), plastic frame and plastic back.The phone display has a resolution of 1080×2400 pixels(394 ppi density)
